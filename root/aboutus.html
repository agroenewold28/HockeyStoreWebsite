Our History

Alex's Hockey Shop was founded in 2014. It started out as something very little but has now grown into one of the largest and most significant hockey gear distributors. Alex's Hockey Shop has now provided for a number of notable NHL Alumni and current NHL superstars. The company sells all hockey gear for skaters and goalies.


The shop has went through a number of struggles. In 2015, the company almost when out of business. Luckily the shop recieved donations from a number of it's former and current customers.


Alex's Hockey Shop is comitted to providing all skil levels with premier Hockey gear!
