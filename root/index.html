Our Mission

Here at Alex's Hockey Shop, we compete to be the top competitor among all others. We want the best on ice performance out of you, and we know you do too. For years we have provided gear for NHL players like Patrice Bergeron. We are comitted to providing all ages and all skill levels with premier hockey equitment. Our hockey equitment is all professionally made and spent much time on. If you are not completely satisfied with our gear, you can return anything full price, no questions asked. When it comes to Ice Hockey, Alex's Hockey shop is no doubt the place to be.
